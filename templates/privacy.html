Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your account is protected by a password; please choose a strong one and keep it confidential.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure (such as hosting and databases). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across sites.

5. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account data at any time through your profile settings. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us and we will process your request promptly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact details provided on our site. We are committed to addressing your concerns in a timely manner.
